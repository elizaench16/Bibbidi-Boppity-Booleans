A glowing engraving appears on the wall:

"Some statements contain special logic operators, which compare two statements and are true under the conditions below. Unmet conditions make the entire statement false:

Greater than (>) is true when the left statement is larger than the right statement.

Less than (<) is true when the left statement is smaller than the right statement.

Equal (==) is true when both statements are the same.

Not equal (!=) is true when both statements are different from each other."

Suddenly, the engraving disappears. You sense a magical presence, and you will no longer be able to rely on this engraving.
