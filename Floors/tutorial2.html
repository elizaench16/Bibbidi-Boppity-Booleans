Another glowing engraving appears on the wall:

"Some statements contain special logic operators, which compare two statements and are true under the conditions below. Unmet conditions make the entire statement false:

And is true when both statements are true.

Or is true when at least one of the statements are true.

Either ... or is true when only one of the statements are true. (This is false when both statements are true.)

Neither ... nor is true when both statements are false."
